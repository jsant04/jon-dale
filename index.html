Our Story

A Love Written
in Light

✦ ✦ ✦

What began as a quiet moment — a glance across a room, a shared laugh over something completely ordinary — has grown into the most extraordinary story either of them has ever known. Jon and Dale found in each other not just a partner, but a home. A place of warmth, honesty, and laughter without end.

"In you, I found the person I didn't know I was looking for."

After years of building a life woven from shared mornings, late-night conversations, and all the beautiful, imperfect moments in between — they are choosing each other. Again. Intentionally. In front of everyone they love.
